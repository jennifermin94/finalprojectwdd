W H A T - W E - D O

o r g a n i z e

CORE team oversees all logistical aspects of Passion Fellowship, including planning out Friday Large Groups, mapping out both Fall and Spring Semester Welcome Weeke, overseeing Retreats, and putting together banquets.

p l a n

Plan, plan, plan... the planning never stops! Whether it is coordinating rides for retreats, brainstorming icebreaker games for Large Group, or working out the last minutes details in the schedule for Senior Banquet, you can be sure that you will always find CORE Team on their toes.

c o m m u n i c a t e

To be successful as a CORE Team member, you need to be able to communicate effectively - with your other Team members, with Worship Team, with Class Directors, with Room Scheduling Services, with FLOW team, and with Pastoral Staff. Every detail counts.

k e e p it t o g e t h e r

We like to think of CORE Team as the glue - or tape - of Passion Fellowship. Without the events, planning, and behind-the-scenes organizing that goes on within CORE Team, Passion Fellowship would look a lot different!
